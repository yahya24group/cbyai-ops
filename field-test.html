Driver clock & road model

Southern depot for Abu Dhabi Dispatch AUH vans from Mussafah (ICAD) instead of the UAQ HQ — removes the ~175 km highway deadhead each way.

Road km = straight-line (haversine) × a piecewise winding factor — 1.40 for short city hops (grid detours), 1.18 for long highway legs — times your extra multiplier. Calibrated to the 23 Jun route test's ~48 km/h effective speed. Coordinates are planning-grade (test/approx). Google Maps links chunk into segments to respect its 9-waypoint cap, so every link actually opens.
